Professional Statement

As someone who feels satisfaction in finding what she’s looking for easily, such as locating a book by its Dewey number or picking up a work of fiction that is exactly what she hoped to read, she found herself drawn to library services. Her professional goal is to help others find this satisfaction when accessing information, whether it’s by assisting in finding sources for research, recommending the perfect novel, or building a database that can collect and manage data that perfectly suits its users’ needs.

The Overlooked Ecological Impacts of Physical and Digital Music

This literature review-based research paper explores the recent history of the music production industry and the effects it has and will continue to have on the environment.

Description

This literature review-based research paper explores the recent history of the music production industry and the effects it has and will continue to have on the environment. The assignment was to research an information-related topic and write an 8-10 page paper on it.

Methods

Through research and advisor review, I assembled a timeline of different music formats and drew connections to patterns between different formats and projections for the future following these patterns.

Role

This was an individual research paper.

Learning Outcome Achieved: Research

Rationale

In this assignment, I developed a query, researched literature on the subject, and synthesized the findings into a comprehensive report, offering solutions.

Learning Outcome Achieved: Ethical/Creative/Critical practice

Rationale

This paper allowed me to think more critically about the consumption of music in the past and provides suggestions for the future to help mitigate the future negative impact the industry will have on the environment.
